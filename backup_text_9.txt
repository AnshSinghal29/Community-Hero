220: A: Yes! Haiku is designed for fast categorization tasks. Opus is overkill for this project. Haiku is actually faster (1–2 sec vs 2–3 sec).

222: **Q: What if I run out of free tokens?**  
223: A: 100K tokens = ~50 issues/day. If you submit 50+ issues in one day during hackathon, you hit the limit. Solution: Get a paid API key later (takes 2 minutes to switch).

225: **Q: Is SQLite safe for production?**  
226: A: For a hackathon MVP? Yes. For actual production? Use PostgreSQL. But for now, perfect.
227: 
228: **Q: Can I migrate to Google Sheets later?**  
229: A: Yes. Just export SQLite to CSV, upload to Google Sheets manually. Takes 5 minutes.
